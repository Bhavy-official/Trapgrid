How to Play
TRAPGRID · AI Edition
Objective

Trap your opponent so they have no valid moves left. Every time you move, your old position becomes a blocked cell — permanently. The board shrinks with every turn.

How to Move
1
Your valid moves are highlighted in gold on the board.
2
Click any highlighted cell to move there.
3
Your previous position becomes blocked (grey ✕) — nobody can go there again.
4
Players can only move up, down, left, or right — no diagonals.

Game Modes
👥
2 Players
Two humans take turns on the same device. Green moves first.
🤖
VS AI
You play Green, the AI plays Blue. It uses Minimax with Alpha-Beta pruning — it thinks ahead!
The AI Algorithms
BFS Flood-fill counts how many cells each player can still reach — their "breathing room".
Minimax The AI simulates future moves up to 3 levels deep, picking the path that maximises its advantage.
Alpha-Beta Prunes branches of the search tree that can't possibly be better — making Minimax much faster.
Heuristic Score = AI territory − Player territory + distance bonus. Positive means AI is winning.
Debug Mode

Toggle Debug in the top bar to reveal the full algorithm panel — live BFS territory zones, the Minimax search tree, Alpha-Beta pruning stats, and a real-time algorithm feed showing every decision the AI makes.

💡 Tips
  • Stay towards the centre early — you'll have more escape routes.
  • Try to cut off your opponent rather than running away.
  • Watch the BFS zone overlay (Debug mode) to see who controls more territory.
  • The AI is strong — but it only looks 3 moves ahead. Surprise it!
